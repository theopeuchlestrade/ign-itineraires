# Roadmap

IGN Itinéraires is maintained as a focused, privacy-conscious route planning
and foreground navigation application using public French map services.

## Good Fits

- Reliability and accessibility improvements to route planning and guidance.
- Better responsive behavior across web, Android, and iOS.
- Tests, CI hardening, dependency maintenance, and public documentation.
- Privacy-preserving local features that do not require an account or
  proprietary backend.
- Small refactors that reduce complexity without changing product behavior.

## Discuss First

- New travel modes or third-party service integrations.
- Background or lock-screen location tracking.
- Analytics, crash reporting, telemetry, or cloud synchronization.
- Changes to network hosts, permissions, local data retention, map providers,
  routing providers, or voice behavior.
- Large navigation, architecture, or state-management changes.

## Out of Scope

- A IGN Itinéraires account or backend dependency.
- Real-time traffic guarantees, emergency navigation, or safety-critical use.
- Offline map distribution and offline routing.
- Official support for unrelated hosted deployments or independently branded
  app-store builds.
- Production signing, deployment, incident-response, and observability
  runbooks.

## Maintainer Notes

Release timing, versioning, mobile signing, official deployment, and final
scope decisions remain maintainer responsibilities.
